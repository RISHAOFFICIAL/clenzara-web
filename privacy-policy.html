1. Information We Collect

When you sign up for the Clenzara Pro App waitlist, we collect your email address and optionally your first name. We also collect UTM parameters (source, medium, campaign) to understand how you found us.

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous usage data, including page views, scroll depth, session duration, and affiliate link clicks. This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email to send:

  • Updates about the Clenzara Pro App launch
  • Educational content about parasite cleansing and gut health
  • Re-engagement emails if you've previously interacted with us

You can unsubscribe from any email at any time using the link in the footer.

3. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as you remain on our waitlist or mailing list. If you unsubscribe or request deletion, your data will be removed within 30 days. Google Analytics data is retained for 26 months before automatic deletion.

4. Your Rights (CCPA & GDPR)

If you are a California resident (CCPA) or an EU resident (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete your personal data.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell your personal data, but you may still opt out of communications.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@clenzara.com. We will respond within 30 days.

5. CAN-SPAM Compliance

We comply with the CAN-SPAM Act of 2003. Every email we send includes:

  • A clear and conspicuous unsubscribe link
  • Our physical mailing address
  • Accurate subject lines that reflect the content

6. CCPA Notice (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

  • Request disclosure of categories of personal information collected
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)

To submit a CCPA request, contact hello@clenzara.com with the subject line "CCPA Request".

Your email and signup data are stored in Google Sheets with access limited to the Clenzara team. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.
